# Mindfulness & Creativity
 
New Year 6 week Mindfulness & Creativity course, Thursday mornings 10am - 11.30am online

Morning meditation, Thursdays weekly 7.30am - 8am online

For further information, please contact sophie(@)gearmill.org

Sophie has a Post Graduate Diploma in Mindfulness Based Approaches from the University of Exeter ( 2012 ), and is a team member with Mindfulness Cornwall CIC.

She trained with the Mindfulness in Schools Project, and at the Oxford Mindfulness Centre, and has led courses for staff and students at Falmouth University, and in local schools. She was also invited to offer mindful movement sessions for MBCT retreats at Gaia House, 2013-2015.
